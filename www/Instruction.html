Introduction

The Great Lakes Region Inventory and Monitoring Network (GLKN) is part of the National Parks Service (NPS) Inventory and Monitoring Division. GLKN conducts long-term ecological monitoring in the following parks: Apostle Islands National Lakeshore, Grand Portage National Monument, Indiana Dunes National Lakeshore, Isle Royale National Park, Mississippi National river and Recreation Area, Pictured Rocks National Lakeshore, Sleeping Bear Dunes National Lakeshore, St. Croix National scenic Riverway, and Voyageurs National Park. This program is designed to provide park managers with status and trends data on vital signs that represent the health of natural resources in the above stated parks.

Instructions

This visualizer tool allows users to explore and analyze water quality data. It uses data published on the
USGS Water Quality Portal.

This visualizer plots data collected by the GLKN. To run the visulizer, follow the steps below:

  1. Select Park of interest
  2. Select one or more Site of interest
  3. The map will zoom to the last Site selected
  4. Select the plot of interest from the tabs at the top
  5. Select a Parameter(s) to plot
  6. Select Years of interest
